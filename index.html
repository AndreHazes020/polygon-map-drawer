Polygon Drawer

Draw a polygon to see area

How to Use

Drawing a Polygon

Tap the Draw button, then tap on the map to place points. Tap the first point again or double-tap to complete the polygon.

Editing

Tap on a polygon to select it. Drag vertices to adjust the shape. Tap outside to deselect.

Location

Tap Locate to zoom to your current position. You may need to allow location access.

Export

Tap Export to download your polygons as a GeoJSON file.
